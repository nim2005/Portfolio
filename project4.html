Cruelty-free Makeup Campaign

Project Overview

This conceptual billboard poster was created for a hypothetical campaign by Sugar Cosmetics’ “Play” line, advocating cruelty-free beauty. Designed for fast-moving environments like subways or bus stations, the poster uses bold contrast, minimal text, and direct visuals to catch attention even from a distance.

The central illustration reimagines a mascara wand’s stroke as claw marks—visually linking beauty to the consequences of animal testing. This striking metaphor subtly confronts the viewer with the ethical weight behind everyday choices. The tagline, “Choose what you leave a mark on,” was developed to mirror the emotional tone of the visual while maintaining the brand’s confident voice. It encourages conscious decisions without sounding preachy, making the message more accessible.

This campaign was extended into social media creatives to connect with a younger, digitally native audience. Altogether, the work positions Sugar as a bold, ethical brand that merges style with substance.
