Jacquemus Fashion magazine

Project Overview

This magazine spread was a visual exploration of the French luxury brand Jacquemus, inspired by its bold aesthetics, playful silhouettes, and spirited color palettes. The concept aimed to mirror the brand’s identity—fresh, energetic, and quirky—through expressive layout design and vibrant color compositions. I experimented with multiple grid systems, intentionally breaking and bending layout conventions to reflect the whimsical, high-fashion nature of the brand. The project became an exercise in balance—between structure and spontaneity, boldness and clarity.

I gained hands-on experience in editorial design, grid systems, typographic hierarchy, and composition. This piece showcases my ability to translate brand narratives into impactful visual stories, making it suitable not only for editorial use but also for posters, book covers, and framed prints.
