Character Design

Project Overview

Kingdom of Redbulbs is a playful character design project where onions meet chess. Inspired by biomimicry, I studied the anatomy and texture of onions and reimagined classic chess pieces with organic forms and personalities. Each character blends the structural logic of chess with the expressive quirks of natural elements, resulting in a fresh, stylized set of illustrated pieces. This project explores form construction, storytelling through design, and imaginative transformation of everyday objects.

It reflects my approach to character building—where observation, creativity, and visual narrative come together to create something fun, meaningful, and visually engaging.
